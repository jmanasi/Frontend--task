Magnificent Iceland

Iceland, island of fire and ice, has become one of the world's top travel destinations, not only with thrill-seeking adventurers, but also nature lovers looking for something different. Here, you'll discover active volcanoes, geysers, hot springs, glaciers, ice fields, and fjords, for this sparsely populated country, resting at the edge of the Arctic Circle, sits atop one of the world's most volcanically active areas.So come and experience this majestic landscape and picturesque nature.

PLACES TO VISIT

Whale Watching

No matter when you plan to travel, whale watching happens year-round, although summer is the most popular time to see these gentle giants.

Address:Ægisgardur 5, Reykjavik's Old Harbor

thumb_up310

Blue Lagoon

This most iconic of geothermal spas is a must-see tourist attraction.Here,you'll find natural bathing in pale blue water in the shadow of a power station.

Address:Norðurljósavegur 9, 240 Grindavík

thumb_up500

Spectacular Geysers

Strokkur Geysir is the most popular fountain geyser in the country and famed throughout the world.

Address:50-minute drive from Reykjavik

thumb_up554

The Northern Lights

This spectacular natural light show is best admired in remote places and is particularly impressive at times of increased solar activity.

Address:The Northern Lights, Aurora Borealis

thumb_up660

Landmannaulaugar National Park

This is one of Iceland's most popular tourist destinations. The main features of this landscape are the multihued rhyolite mountains.

Address:South of Iceland, 180 kilometers from Reykjavik

thumb_up307

Maelifell Volcano

This is the undisputed jewel-in-the-crown of this wild,rugged glacial landscape with a perfect cone shape

Address:Myrdalsjökull Glacier Park

thumb_up444

Skaftafell Ice Cave

Vatnajökull National Park attract adventurers from across the globe.It consists of Vatnajökull glacier and Skaftafell Ice Cave

Address:Skaftafell Ice Cave,Vatnajökull National Park

thumb_up400

Askja Caldera

Askja is an impressive 50 square kilometers in size.It was partly created by an eruption of burning ash that collapsed the roof of the central volcano's magma chamber.

Address:Northern region of Vatnajökull National Park

thumb_up311

Dettifoss

Dettifoss is Plunging 45 meters and with a width of 100 meters, it's said to be the most powerful waterfall in Europe.

Address:North of Vatnajökull National Park

thumb_up460

Kirkjufell Mountain

The town of Grundarfjördur lies in a picturesque fjord, surrounded by mountains, with Mt. Kirkjufell looming as a striking landmark.

Address:Kirkjufell Mountain, Grundarfjördur

thumb_up410

Gullfoss

The river Hvítá plummets into a canyon, which forms three step terraces, creating a powerful torrent of Gullfoss waterfall

Address:90-minute drive west of Reykjavik
